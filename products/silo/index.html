Silo-as-a-Service

Day 0 security. Deployed in minutes.

Per-Instance Identity

Every workload gets its own Managed Identity. No shared credentials. No lateral movement. Score: 6/6.

mTLS Mesh

Istio STRICT mode. AuthorizationPolicy. DestinationRule. NetworkPolicy. Every packet authenticated.

Key Vault Integration

CSI Secrets Store mounts secrets at pod start. Rotation policy enforced. Zero secrets in code.

2-RG Pattern

App RG + Backup RG per workload. No junk drawers. Clean blast radius.

RBAC Least-Privilege

Cross-namespace denied. Cluster-wide denied. Each master sees only its own resources.

Audit & Compliance

Automated silo scoring. Continuous verification. The standard enforces itself.
